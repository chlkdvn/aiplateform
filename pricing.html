Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. If you upgrade to a Business plan mid-month, the price will be prorated.

What happens if I exceed my usage limits?

Starter and Business plans have soft caps. We'll notify you when you reach 80% of your limit. For Enterprise customers, we offer overage protection with dynamic scaling.

Do you offer discounts for educational institutions?

Absolutely. We support the future of AI research. Contact our academic partnerships team with your .edu credentials to apply for the grant program.

Is my data used to train your base models?

No. We maintain strict data siloing. Your proprietary inputs and outputs are never used to train our foundational models without explicit consent.
